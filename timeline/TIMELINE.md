# Aavegotchi: A Historical Timeline (2020 → 2026)

A curated history of the Aavegotchi ecosystem, from the GHST token launch through the current
community-run / survival era. Tier-1 milestones are listed below; the full structured dataset
(311 events, including secondary blog posts and every AGIP/DAO proposal) lives in
[`timeline.json`](./timeline.json).

**How this was built**
- **2020-2021 (Genesis):** external web research, every date cross-checked against 2+ sources
  (official Medium/blog, CoinDesk, wiki, contract listings). The blog archive does not reach this
  far back, so this era is sourced independently.
- **2022-2026:** harvested directly from the dated corpus now in the Gotchi KB — 194 blog posts
  (`blog.aavegotchi.com`), 100 AavegotchiDAO governance/AGIP threads, and announcement channels.
- **Caveat on early blog dates:** ~100 posts carry a 2022 "published" date because the team
  re-hosted the original *The Curve* (Medium) catalogue onto Ghost; titles tagged `| The Curve`
  are republished originals, so a 2022 stamp is a publish-platform artifact, not necessarily the
  original date.

## 1 · Genesis (2020) — *a DeFi-staked NFT is born*

The "Aave" in Aavegotchi is literal: Aave backed the studio, and the ghost is Aave's mascot.

- **2020-08** 🪙 **Whitepaper + GHST private sale** — Pixelcraft Studios publishes the whitepaper and sells 5M GHST at 0.05 DAI, seeding the project.
- **2020-09-14** 🪙 **GHST token launch** via an Aragon bonding-curve presale on Ethereum (0.2 DAI/GHST) — the core funding mechanism. ([wiki](https://wiki.aavegotchi.com/en/curve))
- **2020-10-15** 🏛️ **AavegotchiDAO's inaugural vote** (Aragon DAICO) passes ~99%, raising the team funding tap to 100K DAI/month — GHST governance begins.
- **2020-11-23** 🤝 **Aave invests in Pixelcraft Studios** (Singapore), alongside IOSG and Noir Ventures.
- **2020-12-11** 🛠️ **Jan 4 Ethereum mainnet launch announced** with 10,000 summoning Portals (CoinDesk coverage) — a plan that gas fees would soon upend.

## 2 · Portals & Mainnet (2021) — *the pivot that saved the game*

- **2021-01-06** 🛠️ **Pivot to Polygon (Matic)** — after Ethereum gas spiked on launch eve, the team moves to Polygon for free, instant gameplay txns.
- **2021-01-29** 🪙 **GHST live on Polygon** (QuickSwap) with liquidity-migration incentives.
- **2021-02-01** 🛠️ **Baazaar marketplace announced** — the native on-chain market for Gotchis, Portals, and wearables.
- **2021-03-02** 🎮 **MAINNET LAUNCH on Polygon — Haunt 1** — 10,000 summoning Portals; players can finally mint and summon Gotchis.
- **2021-04-20** 🎮 **Rarity Farming Season 1** (1.4M GHST pool) — the recurring play-to-earn loop that defined Aavegotchi for years.
- **2021-07-15** 🛠️ **First GBM "Bid-to-Earn" auction** — outbid users earn incentives; a novel NFT distribution mechanic. Opened to everyone by **2021-08-25**.
- **2021-09** 🤝 **First Game Jaam** hackathon · **2021-11** 🎮 **first gameplay alpha + Gotchi Lending** (rent Gotchis for RF yield).
