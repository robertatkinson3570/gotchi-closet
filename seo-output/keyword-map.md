## Where GotchiCloset wins

Three structural advantages over every incumbent:
1. **452 programmatic pages already exist** (302 wearables, 150 sets) with real trait data; competitors have zero per-item pages. The sitemap and JSON-LD fixes make them discoverable; prerendering makes them crawlable; adding live floor prices makes them the canonical answer for "{wearable} price/stats" queries.
2. **Post-migration content vacuum**: the entire top of the SERP for how-to queries was written for Polygon. Base-era guides with 2026 dates win on freshness alone.
3. **Live tools beat prose**: this community rewards calculators/dashboards. Pages that ARE the answer (optimizer, floor tracker, lending analytics) collect links and LLM citations that blog posts cannot.
