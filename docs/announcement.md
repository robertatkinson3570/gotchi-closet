# GotchiCloset Announcement

## Tweet

Meet your Gotchi Companion 👻

A chatbot with a personality built from YOUR gotchi's real traits, age, XP & kinship. Ask it anything, let it chat with other gotchis, and throw it into Roast Arena battles.

Every gotchi can also grow a free Soul. Bond depth comes from kinship, XP & showing up, sealed on-chain with a SoulSeal plus a free Soul Certificate. The Soul transfers with the gotchi if it changes hands.

It all lives in GotchiCloset, which now does full Aavegotchi asset management too: Baazaar, GBM auctions, offers, lending, Forge, Land, and gasless DAO voting, all in one place.

Free for the community 💜 Happy to add more if people want it.

---

## Full list of features

### Marketplace
- Baazaar: buy gotchis, wearables, items, parcels, installations, tiles, portals, FAKE Gotchis and Cards, Forge items, Guardian Skins
- Filters, sort, search, and bulk buy
- Make offers (buy orders) with refundable GHST escrow
- GBM auctions with bidding
- Activity feed (sales, offers, auctions) with status and detail
- Stats: Baazaar and auction volume by window, in GHST and USD
- Get GHST on Base: swap, bridge, or buy with card

### Manage
- Your gotchis, wearables, items, parcels, and balances in one place
- Gotchi actions: pet, skill points, rename, respec, list, pocket withdraw, transfer, sacrifice, use consumables, end rental
- Equip wearables and save outfits
- Bulk list, set a pet operator, open portals and summon
- Lending: rent out, borrow, bulk rent, claim, end rentals
- Forge: smelt, forge, craft and upgrade installations and tiles
- Land: channel and claim alchemica, manage installations and tiles

### Governance
- DAO: voting power, treasury, live proposals, gasless in app voting

### For fun
- Gotchi Companion: a chatbot whose personality is based on your gotchi's traits (and age, XP, kinship); it knows the app and Aavegotchi
- Chat with other gotchis and enter Roast Arena battles
- Gotchi Soul: bond depth sealed on chain, free Soul Certificate, public Verify page, transfers with the gotchi
